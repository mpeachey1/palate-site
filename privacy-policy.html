Privacy Policy

Palate — Last updated May 28, 2026

Palate is a tasting note tool for WSET wine students. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

What we collect

  • Your first and last name, entered during onboarding, settings, or feedback submission.
  • Tasting notes and wine observations, entered by you and sent to AI coaching services to generate feedback.
  • Feedback submissions, which include your name, feedback text, and basic device information (device model, iOS version, app version, session count). Submitted only when you choose to send feedback.

The app does not collect location data, contacts, photos, health data, financial information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

How your data is used

Your tasting notes and name are used solely to provide the AI coaching features within the app. They are not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

Third-party services

The app sends data to the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic — your tasting notes and wine details are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate coaching feedback for known-bottle sessions. Anthropic's privacy policy is at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • OpenAI — your tasting notes and observations are sent to OpenAI's API for blind tasting coaching sessions. OpenAI's privacy policy is at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.
  • Apple iCloud — your tasting sessions are synced via iCloud to keep data consistent across your devices. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Google — when you submit in-app feedback, it is sent to a Google Form. Google's privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

No data is sold to third parties. No data is used for advertising or tracking.

Data retention and deletion

Tasting notes are stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud data, delete the app and then remove its data from iCloud Drive in iOS Settings.

Data sent to Anthropic and OpenAI is subject to their respective retention policies. We do not retain copies of API requests on our own servers.

Children

This app is intended for adults of legal drinking age. It is not directed at or intended for use by anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the developer via the feedback form within the app.
